JLIO – Data Deletion

Users can delete their data directly inside the JLIO app by going to:

Settings → Privacy → Delete Data

This will permanently remove:

  • Anonymous messages
  • Stories
  • Pairing data

Some non-identifiable analytics data may be retained for legal or security purposes.

If you are unable to access the app, contact: support@jl.io

No form is provided on this page — deletion happens inside the app.
